This guide walks you through everything you need to know as a new restaurant tenant at Plaza Latina — from how and when to pay rent, to expressing your brand through signage, to making sure nothing falls through the cracks during move-in. Read through at your own pace, check off your tasks, and don't hesitate to reach out to management with any questions.

1
Rent Payment System

All rent payments must be made through the AppFolio tenant portal — no other payment methods are accepted. Rent is due on the 1st of each month. Receipts are accessible anytime through your AppFolio portal.

💡 Pro tip: Set up auto-pay directly in AppFolio so rent goes out automatically on the 1st — no reminders needed, total peace of mind.
2
Signage — Be Creative, Be You

Your sign is the first thing customers see. We encourage creativity — bold colors, unique fonts, neon, dimensional lettering, murals, and illuminated signage are all welcome. Here's what you need to know:

🎨

Design Freedom

Express your brand fully. Choose your own fonts, colors, materials, and style. Neon signs, hand-painted boards, illuminated boxes — all fair game.

📐

Size & Placement

Signs must fit within your designated fascia area. Dimensions are in your lease addendum. No signage may extend beyond your unit boundaries.

✅

Approval Required

Submit a design mockup (PDF or image) to management for written approval before ordering or installing. Turnaround is typically 3–5 business days.

🚫

What's Not Allowed

Flashing strobe lights and signs on shared walls or the parking lot are not permitted.

🔧

Installation

Use a licensed sign contractor. Proof of insurance is required before installation. Coordinate with management on timing to avoid utility conflicts.

🌟 We want your storefront to stand out. Distinctive, well-designed signage benefits the whole plaza. Don't be shy — make it memorable.
3
Quick-Reference Breakdown
Topic Summary
Lease Start Your lease begins on the date signed. Prorated rent applies if you move in mid-month.
Security Deposit Held by management. Returned within 60 days of lease end, less any deductions.
Operating Hours Thu & Mon 11AM–8PM · Fri–Sun 10AM–8PM. You must remain open during all building hours.
Trash & Grease Use designated dumpsters only. Grease trap maintenance is tenant's responsibility.
Health Permits Obtain all city/county permits before opening. Management does not handle licensing.
Shared Spaces Parking lot, sidewalks, and common areas are maintained by management. Do not use for storage.
Maintenance Requests Submit via tenant portal or call the office. Emergencies (gas, fire, water) call 911 first, then management.
Subletting Not permitted without written consent from management.

Move-In Checklist

Tap each item to mark it complete.

  • Sign and return lease agreement Provide a copy to management and retain one for yourself
  • Pay first month's rent and security deposit First payment is made in person with management
  • Set up AppFolio tenant portal account All rent payments must be made through AppFolio — enroll in auto-pay while you're there
  • Complete move-in unit inspection with management Document and photograph any pre-existing damage
  • Obtain city/county business license Required before opening to the public
  • Pass health department inspection Post permit in a visible location inside the restaurant
  • Submit signage design for management approval PDF or image mockup required — allow 3–5 business days
  • Schedule and complete sign installation Use licensed contractor; provide proof of insurance to management
  • Set up utilities (gas, electric, water, internet) Transfer accounts to your name before opening
  • Confirm grease trap installation and service schedule Tenant's responsibility; keep service records on file
  • Confirm emergency contacts are saved Management office, after-hours line, and local fire/health departments
  • Review and confirm insurance requirements with management Provide certificate of general liability insurance naming plaza as additional insured
